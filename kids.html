Privacy Policy — KAK+TAS KIDS

Last updated: July 2, 2026 · Developer: ORIHAKA TIROMO · App: KAK+TAS KIDS (Android)

KAK+TAS KIDS ("the app", "we") is a fully offline game for children and families. It is free, contains no ads, offers no in-app purchases, and requires no account or internet connection. The app does not collect, store off-device, or share any personal or user data whatsoever.

1. Information we collect

None. The app collects no personal information and no usage data. It does not request your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any identifiers.

2. Data stays on the device

Any game progress or settings are stored only on your own device and are never transmitted anywhere. There are no servers, no leaderboards, and no online features.

3. No ads and no third-party services

The app contains no advertising, no advertising identifiers, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs or trackers.

4. Children's privacy

KAK+TAS KIDS is designed for children and follows Google Play's Families policy. Because we collect no data, we do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

5. Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, we will update this policy and its "Last updated" date.

6. Contact

Questions or feedback: dendencanada@gmail.com
